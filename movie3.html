CRUELLA

MAIN ACTORS: EMMA STONE, EMMA THOMPSON, JOEL FRY
AGE RESTRICTION: PG13
SHOWING TIMES: 12:00pm, 20:00pm
Cruella is a 2021 American crime comedy film based on the character Cruella de Vil from Dodie Smith's 1956 novela The Hundred and one dalmations. The film is directed by Craig Gillespie with a screenplay by Dana Fox and Tony McNamara, from Steve Zissis. It is the third live-action adaptation in the 101 dalmations Franchise and serves as reboot and an origin story for the the title character. Set in London during the punk rock movement of the 1970s, the film revolves around Estella Miller, an aspiring fashion designer, as she explores the path that will lead her to become a notorious up-and-coming fashion designer known as Cruella de Vil.
